# Solar Flux ReFabricated

This mod provides players with tiered solar panels that convert energy by capturing solar power and store it inside their compatible modded devices. These panels can send their produced energy to other compatible devices around, thus becoming an excellent source of energy for the game's early and late stages.

Solar panels at each level have improved performance compared to previous levels. Players can modify solar panels with unique upgrade items to supply power to furnaces, power non connected blocks, charge items in their inventory (within a radius), and pass charge through multiple blocks.

Required:
- Fabric API
